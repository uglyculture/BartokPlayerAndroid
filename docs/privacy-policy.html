Privacy Policy

Bartok Player

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

Bartok Player is a free internet radio streaming app for Bartok Radio (Hungarian classical music). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the app works.

Data Collection

Bartok Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to stream audio from Bartok Radio and to fetch the current program schedule. No user data is sent to any server.

Bluetooth

The app requests Bluetooth permission to detect connected audio devices (headphones, speakers) and display the connection status. Bluetooth data is used only locally on your device and is not collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as sleep timer settings) locally on your device. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. It is a simple radio player with no data collection from any user.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository.
